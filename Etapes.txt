1. Créer un 2ème compte :


Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "https://ivoirestream.onrender.com/api/auth/register" -Method POST -ContentType "application/json" -Body '{"username":"T2M Block7","handle":"@T2M_Block7","email":"Block@gmail.com","password":"motdepasse123","avatarEmoji":"👸🏾"}'

2. Se connecter avec le 1er compte et récupérer le token :


$r = Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "https://ivoirestream.onrender.com/api/auth/login" -Method POST -ContentType "application/json" -Body '{"emailOrPhone":"atomiqueesty@gmail.com","password":"motdepasse123"}'
$token = $r.data.accessToken
$userId2 = "..." # copie l'id du 2ème compte depuis l'étape 1
3. Suivre le 2ème compte :


Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "https://ivoirestream.onrender.com/api/users/$userId2/follow" -Method POST -Headers @{Authorization="Bearer $token"}
4. Vérifier que followersCount a augmenté :


Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "https://ivoirestream.onrender.com/api/users/$userId2"
5. Se désabonner :


Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "https://ivoirestream.onrender.com/api/users/$userId2/follow" -Method DELETE -Headers @{Authorization="Bearer $token"}
